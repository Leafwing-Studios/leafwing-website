About Us

Leafwing Studios is a fledgling Canadian indie games studio, founded in 2020 to make richer, more thoughtful games. Whether we’re making tabletop games or video games, we care about:

  • more sustainable, equitable and sensible business models for game development
  • better design and prototyping practices
  • creating memorable, replayable experiences through emergent design

You’ll catch us blogging about our projects, game design, Rust, Bevy and better ways to create and collaborate.
